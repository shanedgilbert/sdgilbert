Spinsters

(Bicycle Parking Redesign)

Overview:

Spin bikes, the new rentable bicycle at UCSD, were parked in a way that crowded roads, sidewalks, and, ultimately, became hazards. Our group sought to improve the way these bikes were parked and improve the overall pedestrian experience.

My Role:

UX Designer, Interviewer, Storyboarder, Prototyper

Deliverables:

Redesigned parking experience

Tools:

Problem:

Blocking Walkways

Causes danger to all those who share walkways (pedestrians and bikers). These can be fire hazards.

Blocking Entrances

Blocks the sidewalk, vehicles, and handicap access

Blocking Bike Racks

Spin bikes are self-locking. They are an inconvencience to bike owners who lose space to the Spin bike or have to work around them.
