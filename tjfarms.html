TJ Organic Farms

Overview:

TJ Organic Farms is a small business located in Taiwan. As a married couple, they work in tandem with their local community to grow organically with a heavy specialization in avocados. Their main goal is to have a website as a means of communicating their adventure to far reaches of the world.

I wanted to join their team to help spread their renewable resource mantra and tell their adventure to the rest of the world.

My Role:

Lead UX Designer

Deliverables:

Wireframe, Sitemap, Prototype, Hi-fi Prototype, Finished Website
