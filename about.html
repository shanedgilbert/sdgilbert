Who is Shane Gilbert?

Based in the Los Angeles area, Shane is a current student at California State University, Long Beach where he is currently getting a second Bachelors of Science in Computer Science.

In 2019, he graduated from University of California, San Diego with a Bachelors of Science in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Design and Interaction where he learned to think like a designer.

Shane is comfortable with working on any type of project ranging from web-based applications to physical prototypes. He is also prepared to learn new things to fully immerse himself in the project at hand.

In his free time, Shane will be running, biking, or enjoying the company of loved ones. Feel free to contact Shane to learn more about him.
